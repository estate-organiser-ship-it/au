Questions

Good to know

How long does it take?

About an afternoon for the first conversation, including reading the script. Most families do one at a time, when it feels right.

Is my information safe?

Yes. It's a file you fill in on your own computer, or print and fill in by hand. Nothing is stored online or in anyone's cloud, so there's nothing to be breached. Where you keep it is entirely up to you.

Is this legal or financial advice?

No, and that is by design. The kit helps your family talk, decide and write down what was agreed. For wills, nominations and legal documents, take what you decide to a solicitor.

Does it work in my state or territory?

Yes. The conversations apply anywhere in Australia, and where rules differ between states and territories the kit flags it, like the family veto rule for organ donation.

What exactly do I get?

Seven scripted conversations, the Legacy Letter Starter prompts, a 12-item Trigger Events watchlist, a 20-item Silent-Killer Audit, and a one-page Executor Prep Letter template. One fillable, printable PDF.
